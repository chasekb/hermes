## Current synthesis
- Harness engineering is the measurement and orchestration layer around agent runs.
- Loop engineering is the runtime control layer that decides when to act, retry, stop, or escalate.
- The overlap is traces, guardrails, checkpoints, reproducibility, and durable evaluation.
